Organization Documentation

This section contains documentation about the goals, missions, and operations of Luminity as an organization.

Our Mission

At Luminity, our mission is to make programming education accessible to individuals from all walks of life, regardless of their background or financial resources.

Our Values

We are committed to the following values:

  • Accessibility
  • Inclusivity
  • Empowerment
  • Innovation
  • Advocacy

Join Us

Join us in our mission to make programming education accessible to all and build a more inclusive and equitable tech industry. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a beginner coder, there's a place for you in the Luminity community.
